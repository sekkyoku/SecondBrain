## Key Takeaways

- **6x/day frequency cap** — matches CTV Vetting Q&A, higher than Programmatic RFI's 3x/day default
- **iCTV pricing: $32.50-$39.50** — first explicit CPM for all 4 iCTV formats
- **Geo-targeting premium: +$1** — cheaper than behavioral (+$2.50); not broken out in other RFIs
- **40+ FAST channels** — most detailed FAST channel listing across all RFIs
- **60/40 female skew** — more female than the 55/45 male skew in Streaming Capabilities 22-23
- **Competitive AVOD restriction** — no competing streaming platforms allowed on Canela properties
