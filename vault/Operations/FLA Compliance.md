## What is the FLA?

The Fair Lending Attestation (FLA) ensures vendor alignment with:

- **ECOA** (Equal Credit Opportunity Act) — prohibits credit discrimination on race, color, religion, national origin, sex, marital status, age, or receipt of public assistance income
- **FHA** (Fair Housing Act) — prohibits housing/mortgage credit discrimination on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, familial status, handicap

Both laws prohibit:
- **Disparate Treatment** — treating consumers differently because of a protected characteristic
- **Disparate Impact** — policy/practice with disproportionately negative impact on a prohibited basis, even without intent

---

## Prohibited Characteristics (14 Categories)

1. Race or Color
2. Religion
3. National Origin
4. Sex or Gender
5. Sexual Orientation
6. Gender Identity
7. Receipt of income from public assistance
8. Political Affiliation
9. Age (if capacity to contract)
10. Disability / Handicap
11. Marital Status
12. Familial Status
13. Good faith exercise of Consumer Credit Protection Act rights
14. **Obvious Proxies** — traits so closely tied to a prohibited basis they are indistinguishable (e.g., primary language, retirement status, HBCUs, women's/men's colleges, Univision viewership, Ebony readership, AARP membership, religious college affiliations)

> **Critical for Canela:** Item 14 explicitly lists "targeting viewers of Univision" and media with protected-class focus as potential proxy violations. Canela's Hispanic-first content positioning must be handled carefully in COF campaigns.

---

## Digital Redlining & Steering Risks

- **Redlining:** Unequal access to credit based on neighborhood demographics via targeted digital ads
- **Steering:** Guiding consumers toward/away from products based on prohibited characteristics rather than needs
- AI/ML optimization increases discrimination potential — COF requires review of all automated optimization methodologies

---

## Attestation Requirements

All vendors must:
1. Provide **data targeting segments** and **automated optimization details** in RFP submissions
2. Sign the attestation confirming no prohibited variables are used
3. Update attestation annually or when methods change
4. Await HMI/COF SCALE legal approval for any new tactics or data segments

### Legal Language (Required Signature)

> "Supplier represents and warrants that the product or Service delivered to Capital One (or provided on behalf of Capital One) uses no element, feature, or factor that is either a 'prohibited basis' as defined by 12 CFR sec. 1002.2(z) or 42 U.S.C. sec. 3605(a) or a proxy for such a prohibited basis."
