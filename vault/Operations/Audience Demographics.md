## Key Takeaways

- **100% Hispanic audience** across all age groups
- **Median age: 35-49** (bilingual US Hispanic audience; 80% bilingual)
- **Gender split:** ~48% Male / 52% Female (slightly female-skewing)
- **Sweet spot:** 35-44 is the single largest age cohort at **24%**
- **A18-49 concentration:** **76%** — strong for advertisers targeting adults under 50
- **A25-54 concentration:** **64%** — majority of audience falls in prime buying demo

> **Note:** The N1% composition table (if different from standard) was blank in the source data, indicating no significant alternate composition.
