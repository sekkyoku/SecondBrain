## RFI Responses

**Q1. Company overview / mission & values**
> To create a new and unique entertainment media company for Hispanic audiences rooted in innovation through technology and content, while connecting with communities authentically.

**Q2. Comscore subscriber? Target audience? Composition?**
> No (not subscribed to Comscore)

**Q3. Brand safety partner? Pre/post bid capabilities?**
> Protected/Human/DV or IAS for fraud and brand safety. Fraud threshold must fall below **2%** for Canela to consider working with a traffic source.

**Q4. Measurement & tracking partners?**
> Ottera, Upwave, CM360, Nielsen, MOAT, IAS, DV

**Q5. Programmatic capabilities?**
> **PG and PMP.** DSPs: DV360, Xandr

**Q6. Average 30-day monthly visitors?**
> **4.3MM**

**Q7. CPM price range?**

| Format | CPM Range |
|--------|-----------|
| CTV/OTT Pre-Roll & Mid-Roll :15 | $26.50 – $31.00 |
| CTV/OTT Pre-Roll & Mid-Roll :30 | $33.60 – $36.00 |
| OLV Pre-Roll | $19.00 – $23.00 |
| Display Standard Banners | $6.75 – $9.00 |

**Q8. 1P data ownership? Audience details & sizing?**
> Yes. Can identify US Hispanic audiences with highest precision:
> 1. Login data with language preference
> 2. Consumption data across 180 Hispanic sites + Canela.TV
> 3. Married data with 3P providers for in-market/intent
> 4. Can onboard client 1P data; works with 3P for viewability, Nielsen DAR, ROI (brand lift & store lift)
> Sizing: **40MM downloads**

**Q9. OMNI integration?**
> (No answer)

**Q10. Media kit link?**
> WeTransfer link provided (time-limited)

- **40MM downloads** as sizing metric
- Fraud threshold: **<2%** to onboard a traffic source
- No Comscore subscription
- No OMNI (OMD proprietary tool) integration
