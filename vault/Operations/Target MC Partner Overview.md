## Key Takeaways

- **Purchase-based targeting: Yes** — unique data point not in any other RFI; enables retail media alignment with Target
- **Look-alike targeting** confirmed — expands beyond contextual/behavioral listed elsewhere
- **55M monthly reach** with 11.2M on O&O Canela.TV specifically
- OTT CPMs ($35/:15, $40/:30) are **premium pricing** — $5-$10 above OMG/CTV Vetting rates
- **Hispanic Owned** certification explicitly stated
- **DV monitoring only, no blocking** — consistent across all agency RFIs
- **Spanish-only content** — no bilingual/English content mentioned for Target
