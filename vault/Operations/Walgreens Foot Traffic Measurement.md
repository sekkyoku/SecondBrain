# Walgreens Foot Traffic Measurement

> Source: Two Walgreens Upfront 26-27 AV Foot Traffic Measurement files (Canela Media)
> Context: Added value foot traffic measurement partner options for Walgreens campaigns

---

## Measurement Partner Comparison

| Field | InMarket | Foursquare |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Partner** | Canela Media | Canela Media |
| **Measurement Provider** | **InMarket** | **Foursquare** |
| **Budget Minimum** | $200K | $200K |
| **Impression Minimum** | 10MM–14MM | 8–10MM |
| **Flight Week Minimum** | 4 weeks | 4 weeks |
| **Min Locations** | — | **30 locations** |
| **Co-mingle Results** | No | No |

---

## Campaign Parameters

### InMarket
- Each campaign can be measured **individually** as long as each one meets the minimums
- Will need a **clean list of physical addresses** (or a specific brand name)
- *Minimums can vary depending on flight length, number of locations measured, and type of locations*

### Foursquare
- Measurement must be applied to a **single campaign**
- Minimum **30 locations** required
- *Need to confirm based on flight, budget, impressions, and locations whether measurement is feasible*

---

## Key Decision Points

| Factor | InMarket Advantage | Foursquare Advantage |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Impression threshold | — | Lower minimum (8–10MM vs 10–14MM) |
| Multi-campaign | Can measure each individually | Must be single campaign |
| Location flexibility | Address list OR brand name | Needs 30+ locations |
| Confirmation | Minimums are defined | Requires pre-flight feasibility check |
