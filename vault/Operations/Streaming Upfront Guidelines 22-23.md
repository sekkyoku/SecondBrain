| **Makegoods** | Flighting/creative errors; Sales: Andres Rincon, Finance: Julieta LaMalfa | Agreed |
| **FEP for Linear** | Consider FEP for linear under-delivery case by case | Agreed |

| **Post Campaign Reporting** | Right to request anytime; 2-3 business day turnaround; quarterly program-level detail | Agreed |
| **Monthly Billing** | Scheduled pre-campaign; withhold if mismatch; 2-3 day revisions | Agreed |

## Key Takeaways

- Canela **agreed to most HMI terms** with two notable exceptions: minimum **3x/day OTT frequency** and questioned DAR guarantees for all deals
- Industry frequency standard is 2x/stream + 7x/week; Canela requires higher minimum
- 3P behavioral targeting at **$2.50 CPM** passthrough; demo targeting at $0; client 1P at $0
- Delivery target: **80% CTV / 20% Desktop+Mobile** OTT minimum
- 10% DSP/SSP discrepancy threshold accepted
- SSP partner was SpotX in 22-23 (now Xandr/Magnite)
