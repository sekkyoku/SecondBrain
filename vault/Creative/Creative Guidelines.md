# Creative Guidelines

## Key Messaging

- **Responsibility:** ALSAC (R = ALSAC)
- **Requirement:** Include Call to Action for all clickable units

## Recommended Creative Mix

A balanced mix of **:15** and **:30** video assets across OTT and OLV:
- **:30s** → storytelling
- **:15s** → reinforcement and frequency

## Volume

- **Max 3 creatives** during a single flight

## Best Practices

1. Capture attention within the **first 3–5 seconds**
2. Introduce branding early
3. Clear, compelling messaging with a strong CTA
4. Optimize for **sound-on** environments
5. Maintain clarity **without sound** where possible
6. Tailor creative for **family-friendly** and contextual environments

## Known DSP Conflicts — Creative

### PG Deals
Certain DSP settings may impact delivery:
- Creative duration mismatches
- Unsupported file formats
- Strict third-party tracking requirements

Ensure alignment with platform specs and approved video standards to avoid trafficking or delivery issues.

### PMP Deals
Creative delivery may be impacted by:
- Mismatched specifications
- Unsupported formats
- DSP-level settings (duration, file size, tracking requirements)

These affect eligibility and scale in a biddable PMP environment.
