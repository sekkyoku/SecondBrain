# Canela Media

## Overview

Canela Media is a **certified diverse-owned** programmatic media partner specializing in premium OTT (Connected TV) and Online Video (OLV) inventory. They serve Hispanic/multicultural audiences through both owned & operated, exclusive, and partner supply.

## Inventory

- **Formats:** OTT (CTV), OLV Pre-Roll, OLV Outstream, Standard Display, Rich Media, Audio, Sponsorships
- **Serving:** Third-party served (DAI via SpringServe for O&O)
- **Supply:** Owned & operated + exclusive + partner supply
- **Ad Load:** ~15 min/hr (2 min ads per 8 min content block); policy unchanged
- **Availability:** May vary by format, device, and targeting parameters
- **Full site list:** See [[Canela Media Inventory]] (700+ sites across 20+ verticals)

- **Preferred SSPs:** Xandr, Magnite

- **1P Data:** Canela Audience Solutions (CAS) — proprietary

- **Waterfall:** DIO → PG → PMP → Open Auction
